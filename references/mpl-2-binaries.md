# MPL 2.0 (Executable Form and Larger Work)

Source: https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/MPL/2.0/

- Section 3.2 allows distributing Executable Form if you also make the Source Code Form of the Covered Software available.
- Section 3.3 permits distributing a "Larger Work" under your own terms, provided MPL-covered files remain under MPL and you provide their source.
- You must preserve notices, license text, and make the source available in required ways.

Use the official MPL 2.0 text for precise obligations.
